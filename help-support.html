Frequently Asked Questions

You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. You will need to enter your registered email address to receive a reset link.

For students, grades are available in the "Academic Records" section of the Student Portal. For teachers, you can manage grades in the "Gradebook" section of the Teacher Portal.

In the "Assignments" section of the Student Portal, find the pending assignment you wish to submit and click the "Upload" button. This will open a file picker for you to select and upload your work.
